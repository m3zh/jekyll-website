Francesco Verso

Francesco Verso (born in 1973 in Bologna) is one of the most popular and translated Italian Science Fiction writers and Editor of the book series Future Fiction.
He has published: Antidoti umani, e-Doll (2008 Urania Award), Livido (Odissea Award and Italia Award, published in the USA by Apex Books and in China by Bofeng Culture as Nexhuman), BloodBusters, (2015 Urania Award, published in the UK by Luna Pr).
